About

Red Dead Redemption II is a 2018 action-adventure game developed and published by Rockstar Games. The game is the third entry in the Red Dead series and is a prequel to the 2010 game Red Dead Redemption. The story is set in 1899 in a fictionalized representation of the Western, Midwestern, and Southern United States and follows outlaw Arthur Morgan, a member of the Van der Linde gang. Arthur must deal with the decline of the Wild West whilst attempting to survive against government forces, rival gangs, and other adversaries. The story also follows fellow gang member John Marston, the protagonist of Red Dead Redemption.

BlackJack

Blackjack, formerly also Black Jack and Vingt-Un, is the American member of a global family of banking games known as Twenty-One, whose relatives include Pontoon and Vingt-et-Un. It is a comparing card game between one or more players and a dealer, where each player in turn competes against the dealer. Players do not compete against each other. It is played with one or more decks of 52 cards, and is the most widely played casino banking game in the world.


Blackjack, also known as 21, is a popular card game played in casinos around the world. The objective of the game is to beat the dealer by having a hand value closer to 21 without exceeding it. Each player is dealt two cards, and they can choose to "hit" (take another card) or "stand" (keep their current hand). Face cards (Kings, Queens, and Jacks) are worth 10 points, Aces can be worth 1 or 11 points, and all other cards are worth their face value.
