About Us

He frequently reflects on his humble beginnings in Madeira, Portugal, and how that background shaped his character. Ronaldo talks about his ambition to continue breaking records, winning trophies, and maintaining peak performance, even as he ages in the world of football.

Additionally, Ronaldo highlights the significance of his family and their influence on his life, particularly the support of his mother and how being a father has impacted his perspective on success and life beyond football.
