About Cha Eun-woo

Cha Eun-woo, born Lee Dong-min on March 30, 1997, in Gunpo, South Korea, is a talented singer and actor under the management of Fantagio Entertainment. Known for his captivating visuals and versatile performances, he has gained widespread recognition in both music and acting, becoming a prominent figure in the South Korean entertainment industry.
